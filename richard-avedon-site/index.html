Richard Avedon: Redefining an Icon


Known for his portraiture work, Richard Avedon has significantly redefined what an icon is, and how they are shot. Avedon manages to capture motion and emotion simultaneously in his photographs. With over 200 pieces, this exhibition features work from Avedon's early street scenes to his iconic celebrity portraits that introduced him to the rest of the world. This site is an interactive piece that allows the user to also redefine an icon in their own perspective. Click the photos below to start your journey.
